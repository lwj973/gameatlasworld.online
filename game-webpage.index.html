Bloxd.io

Jump through challenging 3D obstacles in this exciting parkour game!

Game Description

Bloxd.io (also known as Bloxdhop.io) is an exciting 3D parkour game where your goal is to navigate through challenging obstacles courses. Test your reflexes, timing, and precision as you jump from platform to platform, avoiding falls and reaching the finish line.

With its colorful blocky graphics and intuitive controls, Bloxd.io offers a fun and challenging experience for players of all ages. Can you master all the levels and become the ultimate parkour champion?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Press Space to jump
  • Use your Mouse to look around
  • Press Shift to sprint (when available)

Tips & Tricks

  • Time your jumps carefully - rushing can lead to falls
  • Look ahead to plan your route
  • Some platforms may move or disappear - stay alert!
  • Practice makes perfect - don't give up if you fail
