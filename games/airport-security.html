Airport Security

Manage airport security checks and ensure passenger safety!

Game Description

Airport Security is a fast-paced simulation game that puts you in charge of airport security operations. Your task is to screen passengers and their luggage, identify prohibited items, and ensure the safety of all flights.

With increasing difficulty levels and a variety of suspicious items to detect, this game challenges your attention to detail and quick decision-making skills. Can you maintain security while keeping the lines moving?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use your mouse to inspect luggage and passengers
  • Click on suspicious items to flag them
  • Make quick decisions about whether to let passengers through

Tips & Tricks

  • Learn to identify prohibited items quickly
  • Balance speed and accuracy for the best score
  • Pay attention to the details in X-ray scans
  • Be thorough but efficient to avoid long lines

Game Features

Multiple Security Stations

Handle different types of security checkpoints with unique challenges

Increasing Difficulty

Face more complex scenarios as you advance through levels

Score System

Earn points for correctly identifying threats and maintaining efficiency
